The algorithm loops through and displays a range of numbers (restricted to positive integers) . In this form you shall first input the amount of numbers to loop through, e.g. 1000. The application will then try to divide through all of the numbers and try to match your input of the first divisor and if it's evenly divisible, then it till will display ”Bish”. Accordingly, it will try to match your input of the second divisor and display ”Bosh”, if it's evenly divisible. In the case of a number is both divisible by the firs AND secound number of input, it will display ”Bish-Bosh”. Go ahead and try!
